This was a entry for the international design competiton of the bamiyan cultural center.Conceived as the new ‘Jewel’ of Bamiyan, the building emerges out of the northwest corner of the site glowing like the long lost glowing eyes of the giant Buddha in the new era. The eyes of the giant Buddhas in their glory days were made of red stone which made them glow in the sunset making the valley very tranquil.
