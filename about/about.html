About Me

I am an architect with 15 years of cumulative work experience, currently serving as a Design Principal at Zephyr Architects in China. I am extremely skilled in design, advanced software workflows, and hands-on technical execution, driven by the objective to work on innovative projects that push the boundaries of traditional architectural practice.

My expertise spans from foundational Building Information Modeling to computational design and emerging technologies. I actively utilize tools ranging from Revit and Rhinoceros 3D to Grasshopper, Midjourney, and Stable Diffusion to visualize and execute complex architectural concepts.

Over the course of my career, I have led concept design and design development for diverse, large-scale projects, including biotechnology hubs, commercial mixed-use centers, and specialized structures. My academic foundation was built at CEPT University in Ahmedabad, India, where I earned my Bachelor of Architecture, and my professional work has proudly been recognized with honors such as the Reddot Design Award.
