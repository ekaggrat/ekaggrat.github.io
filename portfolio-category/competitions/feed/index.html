]]> spire http://ekaggrat.github.io/portfolio_page/spire/ Fri, 18 Jun 2010 06:07:52 +0000 http://ekaggrat.github.io/?post_type=portfolio_page&p=942

The ‘SPIRE ’which literally means a spiral, was inspired from the movement of the Japanese folding fan. The hour and the minute hands of this clock constrain the spiral form at two ends. The spiral form of the clock unfolds and folds in the rhythm of a Japanese fan.

Time on the SPIRE is displayed by a play of mass and void. The base houses all the mechatronics required to run the clock. The base and the blades of the clock can be made from various combination of materials such as copper, stainless steel, wood, acrylic

 

Design team : #Ekaggrat singh Kalsi  #Darshan soni

Result : #Reddot award for interior accessory
