The zephyr office which is was working for decided to move 2 floors below. This called for the interior design of the entire office. I got to do the interior of the reception area which inclued special furniture pieces like the reception desk , the center table and the waiting sofas.

The entire furniture is made out of plyood with a cladding of white corian to match the all white interior of the rest of the office. The book shelfs are made out of 12mm factory cut steel sheets.

The center table is made out of 12mm stainless steel bars witha laminated glass top.
