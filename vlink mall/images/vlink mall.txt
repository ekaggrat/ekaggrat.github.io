V-link is a franchise run by vanke which is more or less like a commercially run community center with amenities like day care center, classrooms , conference centers and food courts. The v- link is located in chanchun ( china ) and acts as a gateway to the residential community which lies behind it. As the region is extremely cold in winter most of the activities are enclosed around the heated atrium. Even the street shops have a indoor street to help people shop indoors in the winter. The form of the building was choosen to continue the style of a existing reception center of the project constructed a few years ago.
